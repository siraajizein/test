About Us

Taariikhda Hayat Market

Hayat Market waxaa la aasaasay si loo daboolo baahida bulshada ee alaabo tayo leh. Wuxuu si tartiib ah u noqday mid ka mid ah suuqyada lagu kalsoon yahay.
Himilada (Mission)

Ujeeddadeennu waa:
  • Inaan bixino alaabo tayo leh
  • Inaan macaamiisha siino adeeg heer sare ah
  • Inaan noqono suuq ay dadku ku kalsoonaadaan
Aragtida (Vision)

In Hayat Market uu noqdo:
  • Suuqa ugu weyn ee deegaanka
  • Mid casri ah oo adeegyo online bixiya
  • Hormuud ka noqda ganacsiga casriga ah
Qiimaha (Values)

  • Daacadnimo
  • Kalsooni
  • Tayo
  • Ixtiraam macaamiisha
Kooxda Shaqaalaha (Our Team)

Shaqaalaha Hayat Market waa kuwo:
  • Tababaran
  • Khibrad leh
  • Diyaar u ah inay caawiyaan macaamiisha
Guulaha (Achievements)

  • Koror macaamiil sanad kasta
  • Sumcad wanaagsan
  • Adeegyo cusub sida delivery
